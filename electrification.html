Commercial Building Electrification at Scale

The goal with this data project is to use the LL84 benchmarking to identify the thermal potential in each of the building typologies. 1. Identify the kBTU’s of gas, oil and district steam used (thermal fuel) in the commercial stock. 2. Allocate the thermal fuel among building typologies. 3. Show ranking of typologies by sf and thermal fule volumn. 3. Show steam in manhattan as a breakout.

What is the total thermal energy as reported through the Benchamrking Law, and how does that compair to Electrical energy used in the same buildings.
